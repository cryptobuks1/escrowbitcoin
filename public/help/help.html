FAQ

  • Escrow & Bitcoin

  • What is Escrow?

    An escrow is a financial arrangement where a third party holds and regulates payment of the funds required for two parties involved in a given transaction. It helps make transactions more secure by keeping the payment in a secure escrow account which is only released when all of the terms of an agreement are met as overseen by the escrow company.

  • What are Bitcoins?

    Bitcoin is a cryptocurrency. It is a decentralized digital currency without a central bank or single administrator that can be sent from user to user on the peer-to-peer bitcoin network without the need for intermediaries. Transactions are verified by network nodes through cryptography and recorded in a public distributed ledger called a blockchain.

  • Why should I use escrow?

    An escrow service is especially needed when making payments, buying things online, doing bitcoin exchanges on forums and similar. Since bitcoin transactions are irreversible many use escrow services. The bitcoin escrow service acts as a neutral third party between buyer and seller when doing business online. It works by safely keeping your funds in a deposit account to protect both the buyer and the seller. The money is placed in the control of an independent third party. This makes transactions more secure by keeping the payment safe until all the terms have been met.

  • Transaction

  • How does EscrowBitcoin.net work?

    Buyer or Seller create transaction with details. Email with login information is sent to both parties. Buyer must login into Dashboard to deposit Bitcoins.

    If Seller complete terms of the deal - Once seller complete the deal, buyer can complete the transaction and Bitcoins are sent to Seller's bitcoin address.

    If Seller fails to complete the deal - Buyer can start a dispute and our support will look into the deal to decide. If seller violated the agreement, money will be refunded to buyer.

  • What happen if buyer doesn't deposit Bitcoins?

    If transaction is not funded, seller shouldn't do anything. We can only protect seller, if transaction is already paid. Unpaid transactions will be closed after 30 days.

  • What happed if buyer doesn't complete the transaction?

    If seller completed his part of the deal, but buyer didn't complete the transaction, seller should contact our support. Transactions are auto-completed after 30 days, so seller will get his money.

  • What happen if transaction is not paid in full?

    If transaction is only partial paid, Bitcoins will be refunded after 30 days. If amount is paid if full later, transaction will be auto-completed in 30 days since full payment.

  • Security & Privacy

  • Do you save customer information?

    We save only information about escrow transaction for 30 days. After escrow transaction is completed, all personal data including emails, escrow details and bitcoin address is removed from our database. We do not store your IP, cookies or any other information.

  • Are my informations safe?

    All your data is stored encrypted in our database and your transaction ID & secret are stored only in PBKDF2 hashed form and are removed from our server after email is sent to you. You should copy ID & secret to text file on your local device and delete an email with escrow informations.

  • Can my escrow transaction be hacked?

    No. Hackers can get control over your transaction only if you share ID and transaction secret. As long you keep them save, your money in escrow is save. It would take over 244,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000 years to crack the ID and secret using only brute force.

  • Disputes

  • How are disputes handled?

    The buyer and seller will both provide testimony and evidence (tracking numbers, pictures etc.). Our support will decide the outcome of the case which may be refunding the funds to the buyer or releasing the funds to the seller.
